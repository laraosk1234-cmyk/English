Dagbókin mín fyrri EGOI

Dagur 1

Today was a very long travel day. I got picked up by Frigg, she is our departure leader. She picked me up around 3:30 am. We went then to get my teammate Ösp and went to the airport. We got to the airport around 5 am. We took the first airplane around 7:30 am to Paris. The Paris airport is so big. From the outside it looks like a spaceship. In Paris we had to wait 4 hours for our next flight. I tried to find some “real” macaroons but they all cost so much. One of the shops had ONE macaroon for 7 EUROS. I was not going to take out a loan to try some macaroons. But I found some knock-off macaroons in a shop. They were very interesting. Then we took the flight from Paris to Milano. That flight was not that long. I also was able to sleep on that flight which was good. When we landed in Milano we had to take around 4 trains to just get out of the airport and to the main train station. Before the train got there we went to eat at Mc Donnalds. Then we took a 3 hour train to Cencenerty. The train was an extra 30 minutes long so we got off it around 2:20 am. Then volunteers drove us to our hotel. When we got to our room it was located and the front desk didn't have a key to our room. So we had to knock for like 5 minutes to wake up the other team in there. When we got inside our room I was knocked out from exhaustion.

Dagur 2

I woke up around 7 for breakfast. I think I have never been so tired in my life. I went before my teammate down for breakfast and tried some of the options. They were not the best. But I found one that was really good. When Ösp came down she was so tired. She had 6 shots of coffee and added honey into that. So she had A LOT of energy for the practice run. The practice was just to make sure everything was okay and everything worked before the competition that is tomorrow. After the practice we went to our hotel and had lunch. That was a very questionable meat and lunch but the potatoes were good so I just ate that. After lunch we had some sponsor activities and the opening ceremony. The opening ceremony was postponed for 2 hours because of the wind. But it was all good in the end. After the opening ceremony we had to say goodbye to our leaders for the next 16 hours. We couldn't talk to them when they got the problems and were translating them from English to Icelandic. But we took the evening slow and relaxed and went early to sleep for the composition.

Dagur 3

I woke up around 7 for breakfast. Me and Ösp went down together. We got to know one girl from Croatia named Ozana. I just had the thing I found yesterday and a cake for breakfast and hot chocolate. Ösp had 4 coffees and a really weird shaped kiwi. We went to the contest hall at 9. I have now learned that I should have studied more for this but I cant do anything now. The contest was 5 hours long. So I sat there for 5 hours just daydreaming and writing stuff down so I wouldn't lose my mind. After the contest I met up with my team leader Arnar. He later told me that Ösp landed in the hospital because she drank so much coffee. I went back to the hotel and had lunch with my roommates who are from Ireland. I tried the pasta and when I tasted it it smelled and tasted like the sea and when I looked closer at the meat it had legs. The pasta had little octopuses in it. It tasted soooo bad. For the rest of the day I just ate potatoes and some snacks I had in my room. After dinner me and some of the other girls watched the second Eurovision semi final down in the lobby. It was so fun getting to know them and watching it with them. Oslo, all the countries were having a cultural exchange. We brought with us Þristar and Nóakropp. Everything was eaten so I think they really liked it. I went to bed relatively late but it was okay because we have a travel day tomorrow, not a competition.

Dagur 4

I woke up at around 7:30 and went down for breakfast at round 8. We didn't really eat breakfast because we got our lunch for that day in bags for the trip. We went and met up with our leaders and got on the bus to go to San Marino. When we got there we went on a tour around San Marino and learned about their history and how they have stayed free and independent. After the tour our leaders treated us to lunch in a very fancy restaurant. I got the chicken which was sooo good. The best meal I have had this entire trip. We walked around San Marino and were admiring the beauty of the land. We met so many Icelandic people there. They were always coming up to us and saying how fun it is to hear the ickandick tung again. We went and looked in many different shops. We went into a dinosaur candy shop. Me and ösp bought a few candies to try when we got back. We took the bus back around 17. When we got back, me and Ösp had dinner. I mostly ate potatoes. In the evening we took it slow and cozy after we had been walking all day and the second competition tomorrow.

Dagur 5

I woke up around 7 and went down for breakfast. There were so many in the breakfast hall and me and Ösp were seated at different tables. I got seated with 2 Germans and a guide. The Germans were named Daphne and Martha. It was so fun getting to know them. They told me they wanted to come to Iceland and see the northern lights and see the nature. In the second competition I did equally as bad as I did the first. Today I opened every app on my computer and read it. I had read our problems so often that I noticed a spelling error in the Icelandic one. After the competition when me and Ösp met up with our leaders they were shocked when I said I found a spelling error. After the competition me and Ösp went out to eat. We were so tired of the Italian food so we went out for hamburgers. These hamburgers were my second favourite meal. We went to shops and bought some things to bring home. On our way back we went to the ferris wheel. It was so fun to see the city from this height. When we got off the ferris wheel it started raining and lightning and thunder started. We were so scared to get struck. It rained so much, the raindrops were so big. Then the raindrops escalated to hail and then back to the raindrops. Our clothes were completely stocked. When we got back to our hotel we were completely soaked. We had to change all our clothes. Then for dinner we went to a sponsor event from Bending Spoons which was held at a really fancy hotel. We got dinner, dessert, drinks, activities and so much more. Most of the night me and Ösp were playing werewolf with some of the other girls. On our way back I got to know a girl and she said she really wanted to try Icelandic drinks and our foods. When we got back to the hotel Ösp went back to our room but I went with the girls from Denmark to the Eurovision party. On our way we had to jump the fence even though the organizers said it would be open. I went to bed at around 1:30 am and then they hadn't even revealed all the scorers. This was one of my favourite days because I met so manny girls and talked with them.

Dagur 6

We woke up at around 8. We went and got some apples and oranges for breakfast. We were going on a trip to Mirabel, which is an amusement park so we got our lunch to take with us. We misstated our first bus. On our way there we realised there was going to be a strike in the trains tomorrow and that we would need to take the train today to make it. When we got to the park we had to figure out when we needed to take a taxi to make all the trains and pack everything. We luckily got to enjoy the park. Me and Ösp went on a few rides. We went on one rollercoaster that went so fast I thought I was going to die. After the park we went to the hotel and pacted everything fast and went to meet up with Frigg. Then we took a taxi to the train station and took a train to the other station. We got something to eat there and went and watered for our train. When the train came we got on it and when we tried to find our seats some body was in them. Then we realised we were on the wrong train and were going the wrong way. We got off the train at the next stop and took a taxi to our bus stop. We waited for our bus for an hour. While we were waiting we sat in a park next to the stop that was filled with mice. I think I counted at least 7 around us. We were then on that bus for 2 hours. After that we took a taxi to our hotel. We got to our hotel at around 1 am.

Dagur 7

We slept in and woke up around 9 am. Me and Ösp went down for breakfast but Frigg wanted to stay in our room and sleep more. We had to run in our room at 11 am. We put our baggage in storage and went out to eat. We went to some restaurant that was not that far from our hotel. I didn't get anything to eat because I was still full from breakfast but Frigg and Ösp did. We then hung out in the lobby of the hotel for a bit of time. We then took a taxi to the airport. We got a bit early to the airport so we waited a bit until we could check in and then we went and got something to eat. I went and got a caesar salad. It was really good but the bacon in it was weird. It was kinda weird to meet so many Icelandic people and hear Icelandic after not hearing it for almost a week. On the plane I watched Lego movie 1 and 2. It was really fun and passed the time really fast. When we landed my mom came and picked me up 30 min after we landed and I got KFC on the way back. I will always be able to look back and see the amazing and fun girls I meet in this competition and be friends for life.
